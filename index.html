Liam was once a player in AI Generated High's junior basketball team since he joined in 1986, and later premier basketball in 1990, and had shown great sustained talent in the sport. As a power forward, he had used his immense build to power through many enemy teams, often scoring clutch points to win many games.

Liam's success continued as he graduated high school and attended AI college, where he had led his team into many appearances at nationals, and as a result he had broken the world record for the youngest MVP of the Year at 23 years old in 2009.

After suffering an injury to the ankle that left him unable to play, he puts his experiences as a player into inspiring many young players to give their all.
